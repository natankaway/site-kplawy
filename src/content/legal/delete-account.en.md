# Account deletion — KplaWY

**Applies to:** KplaWY Instant Replay 22s app (iOS, Android, Apple Watch, Wear OS).
**Controller:** Natan Kaway da Silva Pereira (individual responsible for the KplaWY app).

This page explains how to delete your KplaWY account and its associated data.

## How to delete your account in the app

1. Open the KplaWY app.
2. Go to **Account**.
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm. Deletion is **immediate and permanent**.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we remove the data associated with it:

- Account identification (email, display name, internal identifier).
- Subscription state (free/Pro, trial history).
- Your *Sign in with Apple* token, where applicable — we attempt to revoke your access with Apple at deletion time.
- Technical device metadata linked to the account.

## What stays with you

- **Your clips (videos)** remain **on your device** — they were never uploaded to our servers. You can delete them from your photo library at any time.
- **Backups in your Google Drive** stay in **your own** Drive account. You control them and can delete them directly in Drive.

## If you no longer have the app installed

Email **kplawyapp@gmail.com** from your account's email address requesting deletion. We will verify your identity, delete the account and its associated data, and respond within the applicable legal timeframe.

## Data retained for legal obligations

Records that the law requires us to keep — for example, tax records of transactions made via the App Store or Google Play — may be retained for the legal period even after account deletion, and anonymized where possible.

For details on how we handle data, see our [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy).
